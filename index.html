Projects

Kriya

An all-in-one decentralized exchange on Sui.

Web Design

As a personal project, I redesigned the Kriya landing page with the main goal of turning new visitors into participants of the platform.

Kriya offers lucrative yields and has support from trustworthy organizations in the industry, which addresses two key factors to consider when choosing a DeFi platform: security and money-making opportunities. To highlight this, I feature the partners and liquidity pool yields early on the page, just below the hero section. Further down, you will find information about features that are useful to the more proactive users, along with various types of social proof to reinforce trust.

For styling, I chose a clean and easy to digest layout to contrast the complex features and technology of the platform. In addition to Kriya, I also kept the branding and culture of Sui in mind.

GrailersDAO

A decentralized autonomous organization made of artists and collectors that creates, sells, and collects high-end NFT artworks.

Web Design

Webflow

In another personal project, I wanted to give the GrailersDAO landing page a more modern look.

Most changes focused on styling, designed with a tech-savvy art enthusiast in mind—the ideal community member of the DAO in my view. The layout remains similar to the original, with a few additions like the glossary section to help newcomers better understand their work.

This was my first build in Webflow. No framework was used to create it. I plan to refine it in the future.

8NAP Art

A high-end generative art platform for top artists and collectors on Ethereum.

Web Design

In my first real project, I designed the website for 8NAP Art, including a collection management portal for artists.

The ideal user here is already familiar with related terms such as generative art, NFTs, auctions, etc. With this in mind, less attention has been put on explaining the features (docs are available for these details). Instead, more emphasis has been given to the art side of things. The main goal is to display the art in it's full glory, and to turn bystanders to collectors.

I chose a dark, sleek, and elegant style to match both the founders' preference for a dark-mode website and the project's high-end aesthetic.

The final website was built by another developer. You'll see some differences in the prototype and the actual website.

Pialill

An educator and advisor on the topics of entrepreneurship and business.

Web Design

Webflow

In this project, a complete redesign was made to replace Pialill's outdated website, which then was built in Webflow using the Client-First framework. The main goal here was to generate leads and increase bookings and meetings.

The landing page focuses on her services, social proofs and CTAs — to inform, create trust, and prompt visitors to book meetings.

The target client is often an inexperienced or aspiring entrepreneur. To appeal to this audience, we aimed for a design that blends playfulness with reliability.
